# Рядки що починаються з # ігноруються

apple - яблуко - I eat an apple every day.
book - книга - This book is very interesting.

house - будинок - We live in a big house.
water - вода - Drink more water every day.
sun - сонце - The sun is shining today.
moon - місяць - The moon is full tonight.
tree - дерево - There is a big tree in the park.
friend - друг - She is my best friend.
